Textile Artists on Instagram that you need to follow!

Instagram is a great app for posting pictures and connecting with friends. It's also a great place for finding some really great artists and interacting with a larger creative community! Here are some current Textile artists that are making waves in the instagram community! Check them out and follow them to see their work!

Bookhou

Bookhou is a handmade textile goods business started by Arounna and her husband John. They create screenprint simple, geometric patterns on natural textiles to use for their totes and pouches; These bags are made in house, by Arounna herself.

Follow here!

Yokoo

Yokoo started out selling her knitted items on Etsy. Her chunky knit styles, paired with her unique sense of style and quirky photography set her apart from other knitting shops. She has since expanded to designing textiles.

Follow here!
